How the bill is built (and how rough it is)

Each type carries a rough suffering score per kg weighting the number of animals and the severity of typical farmed conditions. The base is that score × kg × your tax rate. In price mode, weight is inferred from a typical retail $/kg — so cheap-per-kg meats (chicken, eggs) imply more animals per dollar and get taxed hardest.
